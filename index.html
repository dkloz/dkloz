About

    Microsoft Internship - Bing

  • September 2013 - September 2014

    Masters in Computer Science, University of Oxford

Debating

I also like Debating, I was a member of the Deree Debating team for 5 years competing and organizing events.
I was coaching high school students at Moraitis School for 4 awesome years.
I am a member of the Oxford Union.

Coaching

Moraitis School

I had the privilege of being the forensics coach of some very bright students in Moraitis School. This involved teaching speech, argumentation, logical thinking and rhetoric skills as well as creative skills for acting and interpreting literature. This has been one of the most fulfilling experiences in my life and one of the driving reasons for my decision to join academia. I am constantly impressed by ingenuity these kids can showcase.
Recently we won a few tournaments :-)

Competing

Deree College, Oxford Union

I have taken part in many tournaments both in Greece and abroad. I was also competing at the Oxford Union whilst in Oxford.
Some of the tournaments I participated are: Panhellenic Forensics Tournament (semi-finals and top 10 speakers), Deree College Invitationals (finals and top 10 speakers), Leiden Open 2007, Prague Open 2008, Cambridge IV 2009, World University Debating Championship 2009, Vienna Open 2010 (semi-finals and top 10 speakers), Paris Open 2012

Organising

Moraitis School, Deree College, Oxford Union

As a student member of the Deree Debating Club I was in the Organizing Committee of many debating tournaments: Athens Open 2009-2011 (Treasurer), Oxford IV 2013 (Tab), Moraitis Cup 2010 - 2013 (Tournament Director, Tab Master - pic), Many Invitational Deree College Tournaments (Tournament Director), Panhellenic Forensics Tournament (Debate Tab master 2010 - 2013)

Debating has been one of the most fun and beneficial experiences of my life. It helped me remain relevant to current events and learn how to properly argue. I highly recommend you devote some time to it, if you get a chance.
